🩺 Health Tips for a Better Mood

💧 Stay Hydrated

Dehydration can impact your mood and energy levels. Aim for 7–8 glasses a day.

😴 Sleep Well

Try to get 7–9 hours of sleep. A rested brain is a happy brain!

🥗 Eat Clean

Include greens, fruits, and protein. Junk food can worsen mood swings.

🏃‍♂️ Move That Body

Even 20 minutes of exercise releases feel-good chemicals like dopamine.
